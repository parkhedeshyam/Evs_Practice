💧 The Water Cycle
☀️ EVAPORATION
Sun heats water → steam rises
Water from rivers, lakes & seas turns into water vapour and goes up
↓
☁️ CONDENSATION
Vapour forms clouds
Water vapour cools and forms tiny droplets which make clouds
↓
🌧️ RAINFALL
Rain falls to ground
Clouds get heavy and water falls back as rain, snow or hail
✔ Evaporation → Condensation → Rainfall → back to rivers/sea (cycle repeats!)

🔑 Key One-Liners to Remember
✔ Herbivores = Plant Eaters | Carnivores = Meat Eaters | Omnivores = Both
✔ Amphibians = Live on Land AND Water (Frog, Toad, Salamander)
✔ Photosynthesis: Sunlight + Water + CO₂ → Food + Oxygen (happens in Leaves)
✔ Water Cycle: Evaporation → Condensation → Rainfall
✔ 3 Earth Layers: Crust (outside) → Mantle (middle) → Core (centre)
✔ 8 Planets: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune
✔ 5 Sense Organs: Eye (See), Ear (Hear), Nose (Smell), Tongue (Taste), Skin (Touch)
✔ Butterfly: Egg → Larva → Pupa → Adult (4 stages = Metamorphosis)
✔ 3 Seasons in India: Summer (hot), Rainy (monsoon), Winter (cold)
✔ National Animal = Tiger | National Bird = Peacock | National Flower = Lotus
